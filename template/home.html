Updated Privacy Policy for Pic Quest

Effective Date: 9/15/2024

Information We Collect

Personal Information: During signup, we collect your name, email address, educational certifications, and a government-issued ID to verify your identity and qualifications.

User Contributions: We collect images you upload in response to tasks we provide, as well as the accompanying instructions or descriptions that help train our AI.

Technical Data: Information such as your device details, IP address, and platform usage is collected to enhance functionality and security.

How We Use Your Information

Account Verification and User Qualification: We use your educational certifications and government-issued ID to ensure that only knowledgeable users are contributing to AI training, helping to maintain data integrity.

AI Training: Your uploaded images and corresponding task responses (answers) are used to train and refine our AI models.

Communication: Your contact information is used to send updates about your account, platform changes, or task notifications.

Security and Compliance: Personal data is protected with high-level security measures and may be shared with authorities if required by law.

How We Protect Your Data

We use advanced encryption and regularly audit our systems to ensure your personal data remains secure. Access to your information is strictly limited to authorized personnel involved in AI development and platform management.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You can also request a download of all the data we have collected about you. To exercise these rights, contact our support team at support@picquest.online

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for the duration of your account’s activity or as needed to provide our services. Once your account is deleted, we remove all personally identifiable information unless legally required to retain it.

Changes to This Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. Any significant changes will be communicated to you via email or posted on this page. Please review this page regularly to stay informed.

For any inquiries or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@picquest.online




Earn Rewards for Training Our AI – Your Knowledge Pays Off!

At Pic Quest, your expertise is highly valued, and we believe in rewarding you for every contribution. By completing tasks and uploading images, you earn a monetary reward. Each task carries a specific value, starting at $0.5 per task, with your total earnings increasing as you complete more tasks and help train our AI.

How It Works

  1. Complete Tasks: Each task comes with a fixed reward, which is added to your account balance upon completion.
  2. Withdraw Earnings: When you're ready to withdraw, head over to the Withdraw page. You'll need to provide your bank details for us to process the request.
  3. Reward Processing: After you submit a withdrawal request, we may:
    • Transfer the full amount you earned directly to your bank, OR
    • Offer a reward of equal value, such as gift cards or other exciting prizes, sent to your email.

No matter the option, you'll always receive the full value of your hard-earned rewards. We'll send you an email notification detailing your reward, ensuring you're informed every step of the way.

Fair and Transparent

We take pride in being transparent with our users. You will always be notified about your withdrawal status and the reward being processed, with all transactions handled securely and reliably.

Stay Informed

Anytime a reward or withdrawal is available, you'll receive an email notification with all the details. Whether it's cash in your account or an exciting prize delivered to your inbox, your contributions will never go unrecognized!
